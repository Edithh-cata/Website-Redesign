CBT

Welcome to ExcelerateMtc !

The CBT was introduced by the government in 1990. The aim is to make Riding safer,giving you the necessary skills & confidence to ride on the roads. You must have a CBT before lawfully riding on the roads.

CBT is usually a one day course and is not a test, but is a training day which enables you to reach a suitable standard for safer road riding.

What you will need for your CBT:

Provisional/full UK licence. Be able to read a number plate from a distance of 20.5m/67ft (old style number plate) or 20.m/66ft new style number plate. Good knowledge of the highway code.
