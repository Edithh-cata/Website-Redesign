Welcome to our website!

At Excelerate Motorcycle Training Centre, we offer friendly, professional training for all experience levels, from beginners to advanced riders. Our qualified DVSA-approved instructors are here to provide safe, enjoyable lessons tailored to your needs.

We welcome both male and female riders, offering lowered motorcycles and a fully qualified female instructor for CBT and direct access training. With access to well-maintained training grounds and competitive pricing, we're passionate about helping you succeed. Flexible payment options are available for all courses.

Feel free to check our locations and find the nearest one to start your journey with us!

Take the first step towards mastering the road with our unbeatable motorbike training plans. Let's ride, learn, and conquer the streets together!
