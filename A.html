This license is designed for individuals aged 24 and over, or those aged 21 with at least two years of experience holding an A2 license.

Once obtained, you will be entitled to:

Ride a motorcycle of any size or power. Carry pillion passengers. Ride without L plates.

If this is your first motorcycle test, you will need to complete a motorcycle theory test. For your convenience, we can provide a practice DVD to help you prepare.

This license offers full riding freedom and is the ultimate step for any aspiring motorcyclist.
