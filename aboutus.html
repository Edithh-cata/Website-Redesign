About Us

Excelerate Motorcycle Training Centre is a purpose-built facility, established in 2010, to provide expert motorcycle training to clients across the UK.

As fully qualified and DVSA (Driving Vehicle Standards Agency) monitored instructors, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality training with a personal touch. Our small, welcoming team is dedicated to tailoring our training programs to suit riders of all levels, from complete beginners to those returning to motorcycling after a break. We offer professional and friendly instruction for both male and female riders, and are proud to now have a qualified female CBT (Compulsory Basic Training) instructor on board.

Our CBT courses are conducted primarily in the Farnborough area of Hampshire, with training taking place at both the Wavell School in Farnborough and the Mytchett Community Centre in Surrey (140 Mytchett Road, Mytchett, GU16 6AA). The on-road portion of our courses covers roads in and around Farnborough, often including routes through Aldershot, Ash Vale, Mytchett, Frimley, Fleet, and other surrounding areas.

For our Restricted and Direct Access courses, we use similar routes, incorporating both country lanes and main roads around Guildford and nearby locations. Depending on weather conditions, we may also take a break at popular stops like the Alton Café, Newlands Corner, or Box Hill. Being located just minutes from the Farnborough Test Centre, we ensure our students are familiar with the routes used for the Module 2 test, further preparing them for success.

We are proud to be registered with respected trade associations, including:

Pulse Motorcycles
Lexmoto
Snells Ducati - Alton

Our facility operates seven days a week, from 8 am to 8 pm, offering flexible scheduling to meet your needs. At Excelerate, we're committed to ensuring every rider feels confident, capable, and ready to ride safely on the road.
