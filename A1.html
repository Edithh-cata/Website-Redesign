MOTORCYCLE TRAINING CENTRE FARNBOROUGH / MYTCHETT

A1

This license is designed for individuals aged 17 to 19 (and older), offering a comprehensive 4 to 5-day course tailored to your ability and prior riding experience.

Upon successful completion, you will be qualified to ride motorcycles with the following specifications:



Small motorcycles up to 125cc with a maximum of 14.6bhp/11KW (or 10.1bhp/7KW). Please note: You will need to pass a theory test as part of the process.

Once you've earned this license, you'll be able to:

Ride a 125cc motorcycle without L plates.

Carry pillion passengers.

After two years of riding experience, you'll be eligible to advance to an A2 or A license, depending on your age. The good news? You won't need to retake the theory test—just the practical exams.

This course offers a smooth progression, ensuring you're fully prepared for the road and any future licenses you may want to pursue.
